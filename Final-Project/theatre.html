I discovered the power of theatre and performance when I was young. Like a lot of kids, my parents signed me up for dance at the age of 4
and this small act set me on a path towards pursuing theatre as a career. Throughout my childhood and into my teen years I spent much of
my free time attending dance, voice, acting lessons, and performing on stage. I met some of my nearest and dearest friends through these
activities.

After being an active member of my high school's theatre department and drama club, I chose to pursue a theatre degree from Long Island
University. I gained a wealth of knowledge during my one year in the program. While my life has taken a different path, I am still very much
involved in local community theatres in both Lancaster and Lebanon counties. In addition to performing at these theatres, I have served as
Vice President of the Board of Directors at Lebanon Community theatre and as part of the directing team for Manheim Performing Arts
Community Theatre. You can find more information on these positions under the Volunteer tab.
