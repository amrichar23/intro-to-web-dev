HELLO



I am Amanda Richardson! Originally from New Jersey, I know reside in Lancaster County with my husband, daughter, two dogs, and four frogs. I am
currently studying social work at Millersville University. Upon graduating, I plan to pursue a master's degree, with a focus on mental health and drama.
After that, I plan to become certified in Expressive Arts Therapy, with the hopes of using drama therapy techniques in my practice. Theatre has always
been a huge force in my life. I truly believe that access to performing arts provides a safe environment to explore one's emotions and identity. Drama
therapy is increasing in popularity and has been show to be an effective intervention when working with various populations of all ages. I hope to bring
my theatre experience along with me into my professional career, to provide the best course of treatment for my future clients.

It has always been a dream of mine to start a nonprofit organization that brings theatre to individuals living with developmental disabilities. My goal is
to offer year-round, week long theatre camps for all ages! I believe that my social work background in expressive arts therapy will allow me to provide
a really enriching program to the community. Check back for updates!

For more information on my experience on and off the stage, I invite you to check out the Experience tab.
